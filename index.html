About us

Borrowed Kitchen Bakery and the Farmhouse Cafe is owned and operated by Lacey and Kory Anders. We started our business in 2010 at the Poulsbo Farmer's Market as Borrowed Kitchen Bakery because we borrowed commercial kitchens in the area in order to bake all of our goods. after several years of markets we opened a small storefront in 2012. Since then, we have grown and are please to have added a full service cafe. The Farmhouse Cafe takes it's name from our 1890's farmhouse in which we live, here in Kingston. It is also a style of food that we are cooking. A back to your roots, comfort, with real honest ingredients, kind of cafe. In the bakery we strive to bring our customers high quality baked goods at a fair price, while still making everything from scratch, by hand, daily for the freshest baked goods experience you can have. The Farmhouse Cafe is equally as set on serving our customers high quality, from scratch comfort food. We make everything here in house, from roasting our own meats, to making our own sauces, jams, and soups. Look for our specialty goods sold in our display case under Farmhouse Foods. We hope you enjoy a little of our worlds when you visit us in our store.
